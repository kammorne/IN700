This semester, most of my development time has gone towards creating a webapp to display all of the data we have from the nodes we've created previously. This webapp has the capibility to display the latest 100 entries from any of our nodes, and allows the users to look at a single node's data. The future plans for this app are to have google authentication for logging in, which will link in to allowing users to create new nodes under their login, and then have that user be able to see only their nodes if they wish, or they can look at the other nodes data like they already can.
